Skills development projects of the WorldSkills Foundation are driving regional economic progress: employability, growth, and sustainability.

The WorldSkills Foundation was established in 2011 with the aim to bring together the unique skills, expertise, and passion of our global network to facilitate projects that positively impact local communities around the world.
