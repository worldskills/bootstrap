Skills drive economic progress.

The WorldSkills Foundation drives regional economic progress through skills development projects all over the world. With its projects, it raises awareness for the impact of education as the key factor for innovation and success.

About us

Make an impact

Innovate and drive skills development in your region

Together with the experts of the WorldSkills Foundation and its education network, companies and institutions create measurable and sustainable progress. Together we deliver skills projects in globally relevant sectors, aligned with the United Nations sustainable development goals.

Strengthen your learning ecosystem

Our partners build their corporate learning ecosystems to achieve sustainabilty and innovation, and to prepare their workforce for current and future technology challenges.

And, together, we promote life-long learning as the key factor for progress and success. Our goal is to ensure the quality of learning ecosystems, to innovate learning methods and technologies, and to support our partners as successful drivers of employment and skills development in local markets.
